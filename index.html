Kelly Kim (she/her) is a multi-disciplinary NFT movement artist based in Mexico City specialising in vogue femme and house.

Her introduction to movement was serendipitous. Starting out in vogue femme, she found a home within the ballroom community in Sydney Australia in 2019, co-founding the Kiki House of Silky. She joined the International House of Amazon that same year. She has since taught classes/workshops in Australia, Thailand, Portugal and New Zealand.

Movement to her is healing through art, and the loudest form of protect. She focuses on somatic awareness, telling stories with the corporeal vessel believes that movement is a force to be reckoned with in a forward-thinking society. Currently, she is focusing on migrating her art presence the blockchain, learning how to move in the language of Gaga, as well as various performance art projects.

You can support her on Foundation, Rarible, OpenSea and Hic et Nunc.
